Hi Travellers,

Though this is a technical blog, I thought of helping people, who are travelling from India to Singapore under VBM (Vande Bharat Mission). My wife is one of the traveller.

Here is the list of frequently asked questions.

*(Please comment if you find something to be corrected, or added in this post. I’ll keep updating this post with more information.)

Checklist to carry FAQs

Congratulations on getting approval from MOM after multiple rejections and a flight ticket of course. Here is the checklist to carry before you start your journey.

  1. Passport (Should be valid for at least 6 months at the time of travelling)
  2. Valid EP/DP/IPA
  3. Printout of Flight Ticket
  4. Printout of the MOM Entry Approval Email
    *(Required at Singapore Airport)
  5. Printout of SHN Facility Sharing Approval Email
    *(Required at Singapore Airport)
  6. Printout of SG Arrival Card - Health Declaration
    *(Required at Singapore Airport)
  7. Printout of Health Declaration Form
    *(Required at India Airport, Download using this link, Also will be provided at the airport)
  8. Printout of Indemnity Quarantine Cost Form
    *(Required at India Airport, Download using this link, Also will be provided at the airport)
  9. Web Check-in and Boarding Pass
    *(Required at India Airport)
  10. Download & Install Arogya Setu App
    *(Required in India)
  11. Download & Install Homer App
    *(Required in Singapore)
  12. Download & Install TraceTogether App
    *(Required in Singapore)
  13. Valid Singapore Phone number
    *(Which you have given at the time of Entry approval application)
  14. Thermometer
    *(You’ll need this in hotel to measure your temperature everyday)
  15. Mask & Hand Sanitizer
    *(For your safety)
  16. Pen & few A4 Size Papers
    *(You’ll need this at both the airports)

Taking printout of approvals and forms is not mandatory and soft copy also works. I personally recommend to take printout for safer side.

Flight Ticket Booking FAQs

  1. You can regularly check Air India Website Page for Evacuation Flights http://www.airindia.in/evacuation-flight.htm
  2. Check the upcoming flights available and apply for entry approval for that particular date
  3. It is recommended that you book the flight ticket after getting entry approval. Though it is completely your choice.
  4. Send a copy of flight ticket to your employer via mail once booked.
  5. If you cancel the flight ticket then you should get the refund back after deducting cancellation charges within 2-4 months. You can call to customer care for cancellation and additionally can write an email to eCommerce@airindia.in and contactus@airindia.in stating the reason of cancellation.

MOM Entry Approval Application FAQs

  1. You cannot apply for yourself and your dependents. You need to request your employer to do so.
  2. MOM generally takes one day to reply. They inform the result to your employer via mail.
  3. There are chances that your application would be rejected multiple times. Don’t loose hope. Keep trying. Keep requesting your employers to apply.
  4. Travel date need to be mentioned in the application. Approval is valid for +-1 days i.e. If you have applied for the 22-July-2020 date then you can enter Singapore in between 21-July-2020 to 23-July-2020.
  5. You need to bear the total cost of $2200 for a 14-day stay at a dedicated SHN Hotel facility ($2000) and Covid-19 test ($200)

Special SHN Request FAQs

  1. You can apply for sharing SHN facility request or any other special request using this link: https://form.gov.sg/#!/5e79ee694c5bc7001181ce00
  2. If you require Separate SHN Facility such as Home stay because of medical or another critical condition then your employer need to apply for this request. Your HR will write mail to mfa@mfa.sg for this. Though there are very rare chances of getting approval until unless any extraordinary case.

SG Arrival Card - Health Declaration FAQs

  1. It is mandatory to fill in Health Declaration form and carry printout - https://eservices.ica.gov.sg/sgarrivalcard/
  2. You can fill the form within 3 days before arrival to singapore.
  3. This is the address you should fill in the form:
    Others ➔ SHN Dedicated Facility

Inter State Travel FAQs

  1. You can apply for state wise e-pass for inter-state movement permission in case you are reaching at the airport from another state using this link: http://ficci.in/sector/130/add_Docs/State-wise-Links-for-e-passes.PDF

Baggage Allowance FAQs

  1. Baggage for international flight is 30KG Check-in and 7KG Hand Luggage. 10KG Check-in baggage is allowed on Infant ticket. Maximum 2 nos of baggages can be Checked-in and 1 in Hand Luggage. Excess Baggage is allowed and cost INR 1500 Per KG.
  2. Baggage for domestic flight is 20KG Check-in and 7KG Hand Luggage. Baby stroller is allowed as long as it is foldable (No Charge)

India Airport FAQs

  1. Process: At the terminal gate you have to fill ( two health declaration form & two deportation form). Temperature will be recorded on your health declaration form. Passport and Ticket check. Enter the gate and have to proceed to a counter where you have to submit the health declaration form. Go to the baggage counter – normal procedure hereafter.
  2. Web Check-in is Mandatory for Air India Flight. Apply within 48 hours before the travel date using this link: https://ota.airindia.in/vandebharat/PassengerHealthUndertaking.aspx
    Sometime Web Check-in fails or show error. Call customer care for assistance.

In-flight Experience

  1. Those allocated middle seat has to wear full PPE suite provided by airlines
  2. Rest all have to wear face shield along with mask
  3. Food packet were kept at your seat which included veg sandwich, snacks and water bottle
  4. Extra water bottle were kept at the tail section near to toilet
  5. No or less interaction with the crew

Singapore Airport FAQs

  1. Process: At the immigration hall first they will do the introduction where SHN rules will be explained. Go to the officer and show your document where they will check your boarding pass, mom pre-entry approval letter and health declaration form. Different counters are setup and you will be asked to stand in a queue. You have to provide your mobile number and they will send you a message to check your number is working or not. At the counter they will verify your document and record it in there register and provide you a Hotel sticker which you have to stick on your cloth. Proceed for the immigration. After that go to the baggage belt, your bags were already kept there. Collect your bags and proceed to the hotel counter. Register your name and wait until they form a group. A ambassador officer will be assigned to a group and he/she will take you outside the airport towards bus. They will load your luggage in the bus. You will not get to know which hotel you are going to until you reach hotel lobby.
  2. You can apply for the porter service at the Singapore Airport in advance if you need any baggage related assistance. Charges are around $3-$10. Make reservation at least 8 hours before prior to arrival using this link: https://smartecarte.com.sg/portal/

Singapore Hotel Experience

  1. Check-in was smooth and rooms are already allocated.
  2. Three course set menu is provided which includes Western, Local, Chinese & Veg options. Few hotels also have kids menu.
  3. You will receive daily one or two calls from MOM from your second day onwards.
  4. From second or third day you will receive message from MOM to download Homer application.
  5. You have to daily submit selfie and temperature 3 times in a day.
  6. WiFi is provided.
  7. On your 11th day you have to go for swab test. You will be informed via sms about the location, date and time.
  8. You have to book cab only from the list of numbers provided. No need to book two way cab as it might take an hour to do the test depending on queue.
  9. Test is done via both nostrils and it is painless. So not to worry.
  10. You will get the results via sms within 48hrs.
  11. You cannot meet your family members during your stay at the hotel. Though your family member can deliver some items at the hotel reception for you.
