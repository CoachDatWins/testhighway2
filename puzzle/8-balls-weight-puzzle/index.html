8 Balls Weight Puzzle

April 22, 2012
Puzzles

Problem

You have 8 balls. One of them is defective and weighs less than others. You have a balance to measure balls against each other. In 2 weightings how do you find the defective one?

Solution

Number the balls 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8

Start off with them in 3 groups: [1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6] and [7, 8] Keep [7, 8] aside and weigh [1, 2, 3] and [4, 5, 6] with 3 possible outcomes:

If they balance then 7 or 8 is defective ball, weigh [7] and [8] with 2 possible outcomes:
  1. If 7 is heavy then 8 is the defective one
  2. If 8 is heavy then 7 is the defective one
If [1,2,3] < [4,5,6] then 1, 2 or 3 is defective ball, keep 3 aside and weigh [1] and [2] with 3 possible outcomes:
  1. If 1 and 2 balances, then 3 is defective one
  2. If 1 is heavy then 2 is the defective one
  3. If 2 is heavy then 1 is defective one
If [1,2,3] > [4,5,6] then 4, 5 or 6 is defective ball, keep 6 aside and weigh [4] and [5] with 3 possible outcomes:
  1. If 4 and 5 balances, then 6 is defective one
  2. If 4 is heavy then 5 is the defective one
  3. If 5 is heavy then 4 is defective one
